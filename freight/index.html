The Process

How the Platform Works

Post your load, receive a competitive bid from BIH, accept and track — all in one place.

01

Post Your Load

Enter origin, destination, equipment type, pickup date, and your target rate. Takes 2 minutes and your load is live immediately.

02

BIH Reviews & Bids

Our team sees your load instantly and submits a competitive bid. You'll be notified the moment a bid comes in — no waiting on phone calls.

03

Accept the Bid

Review BIH's rate and accept with one click. Rate confirmation and paperwork are generated automatically — no back-and-forth emails.

04

Track & Invoice

Real-time status updates from pickup to delivery. Invoices are generated on delivery and commissions calculated automatically.
