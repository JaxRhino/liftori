The Story

Welcome to V.J. Thrift Finds — where vintage junk becomes timeless treasure. I'm Vanessa, and I hunt thrift shops and garage sales across Florida, searching for unique, beautiful pieces that deserve a second life. Every item in our collection has been carefully selected, cleaned, and curated with love.— Vanessa, Founder

What started as a passion for discovering hidden gems has become a mission to rescue forgotten treasures and give them a new home. I believe the best things in life have a story — and every piece here has one waiting to be written in your space.

Our Mission

V.J. Thrift Finds is dedicated to sustainable shopping and the art of vintage discovery. We're passionate about reducing waste, supporting local thrift communities, and connecting beautiful vintage pieces with people who will treasure them. In a world of fast fashion and disposable goods, we celebrate the beauty, quality, and character of timeless design.
