How to Buy

How do I browse and purchase items? ▼

Shopping with us is simple and enjoyable:

  • Browse the Shop: Explore our curated collection of vintage and thrifted treasures organized by category.
  • View Details: Each item has photos, descriptions, dimensions, condition notes, and pricing.
  • Add to Cart: Click the "Add to Cart" button to save items for purchase.
  • Checkout: Review your cart and proceed to secure checkout with Stripe.
  • Confirmation: You'll receive an order confirmation email with tracking information once your item ships.
Can I see items before they ship? ▼

Each product listing includes detailed photos from multiple angles, condition descriptions, and measurements. We take pride in providing accurate representation of every piece. If you have specific questions about an item before purchasing, please contact us at VJ@VJthrifts.com.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept all major payment methods through Stripe, including:

  • Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • Debit cards
  • Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)

Payment is required at checkout before we process your order.

Is my payment information secure? ▼

Yes. We use Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 compliant payment processor, to handle all transactions. Your payment information is encrypted and never stored on our servers. Stripe is trusted by millions of businesses worldwide for secure payment processing.

When will I be charged? ▼

You will be charged at checkout immediately when you complete your purchase. Your order will be confirmed and prepared for shipment right away.

Shipping Policy

How long does shipping take? ▼

We aim to ship all orders within 7 business days of purchase. Once your item ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email so you can monitor its progress. Please allow up to 2 weeks for delivery once shipped, depending on your location and carrier.

Do you ship internationally? ▼

We currently ship throughout the United States and select international locations. Shipping costs will be calculated at checkout based on your location and item weight.

What shipping method do you use? ▼

We use the most affordable and reliable shipping option available for your order. Most items are shipped via USPS or UPS with tracking included. You'll receive a tracking number once your item is in transit.

Do you offer expedited shipping? ▼

We prioritize using the most cost-effective shipping options to keep prices reasonable. Contact us at VJ@VJthrifts.com if you need expedited shipping options for your order.

Can I track my order? ▼

Yes! Once your item ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package with the carrier (USPS, UPS, etc.) in real-time.

Damaged Items

What if my item arrives damaged? ▼

We carefully package each item to ensure it arrives in perfect condition. However, accidents can happen during shipping. If your item arrives damaged:

  • Message us immediately with clear photos of the damage and packaging
  • We will help you file a claim with the carrier
  • Keep all packaging materials for the claim process

Important: V.J. Thrift Finds is not responsible for damage that occurs during transit. Carrier insurance applies in these cases.

Can I get a refund for a damaged item? ▼

Transit damage claims are handled through the shipping carrier's insurance. We will assist you in filing the claim, but refunds and replacements are determined by the carrier based on their policies. We work hard to ensure items arrive safely, and we're here to support you through the claims process.

Return Policy

What is your return policy? ▼

All vintage and thrifted items are sold as-is. Since each piece is unique and one-of-a-kind, we do not offer returns or exchanges. We provide detailed photos, measurements, and condition descriptions for every item to help you make informed decisions. If you have questions before purchasing, please reach out!

What if the item isn't what I expected? ▼

We provide comprehensive product information including multiple photos from different angles, dimensions, condition notes, and detailed descriptions. If you have concerns before purchasing, contact us at VJ@VJthrifts.com. For items with condition issues not disclosed in the listing, please message us with photos and we'll work with you to find a solution.

Privacy Policy

How do you handle my personal information? ▼

We take your privacy seriously. Here's how we handle your information:

  • Collection: We collect only the information necessary to process your order (name, email, address, phone) and improve your shopping experience.

  • Sharing: We never sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Can I request my data? ▼

Absolutely. You have the right to request, access, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at VJ@VJthrifts.com with your request and we'll respond within 30 days.
