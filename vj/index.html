Where Old Finds New Life

Every piece in this shop has been hand-picked from thrift stores, estate sales, and garage sales across Florida. What some see as junk, I see as treasure waiting for the right home.

From vintage lamps and antique glassware to one-of-a-kind decor pieces — each item carries its own history. My job is to rescue them, clean them up, and connect them with someone who’ll love them as much as I do.
