## Introducing the Compound Path

The Compound Path is Liftori's answer to the single biggest objection in startup fundraising: *"I don't have the money."*

We believe that if you're willing to believe in a founder, you shouldn't need a six-figure bank balance to back them.

### Here's how it works, in plain English

You sign **one** master agreement today. You choose a monthly contribution tier. Our platform auto-debits that amount from your bank account every month for 12 months. Every month, that contribution buys you real equity in Liftori at **today's locked-in valuation cap** — not whatever the valuation is a year from now.

At the end of 12 months, you own a real position in the company, built one small contribution at a time. You never had to "find" the $25K. You reallocated what you were already spending on stuff you won't remember in five years.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I cancel?**
Yes — anytime. Your existing equity stays yours. Future debits stop the moment you cancel.

**What if I want to contribute more than $2,500/mo?**
The Champion tier is the top of the standard menu, but if you want to go higher or write a traditional SAFE check, reach out — we'll set up the **Founders' Circle** path for you (accredited investors only, $25K+).

**Is this secure?**
Yes. The Compound Path is structured as an SEC-compliant offering. For non-accredited investors, we use Regulation CF (crowdfunding rules). For accredited investors at $1K/mo and above, we use Regulation D 506(c). Your contributions are held in escrow and your SAFE is filed with the SEC.

**What do I actually own?**
A SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity). When Liftori raises its next priced round or gets acquired, your SAFE converts into real equity at the locked valuation cap — giving you full upside participation.

**When do I see my first check?**
This is a long-term equity position, not a dividend product. If you want monthly cash distributions, check out our **Flywheel Fund** — a separate path that pays out monthly from our processor revenue pool. Many of our backers do both: Compound Path for long-term upside, Flywheel Fund for monthly cash.

**What if Liftori fails?**
Early-stage startup investing carries real risk. The Compound Path lowers your exposure by design — you never commit more than $2,500/mo, and you can cancel at any time. If Liftori fails, you lose what you've contributed; you never owe more. Only invest what you can afford to walk away from.
