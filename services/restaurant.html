Complete digital tools designed specifically for restaurants

Digital Menu Management

Create, organize, and update your menu with photos, descriptions, prices, and dietary information. Categorize by appetizers, entrees, desserts, and more.

Online Ordering System

Accept orders directly from your website with real-time inventory tracking, customizable options, and multiple payment methods. Watch orders flow in 24/7.

Table Reservations

Allow customers to reserve tables online in real-time. Manage availability, send confirmation emails, and reduce no-shows with automatic reminders.

Delivery & Pickup Options

Let customers choose delivery or pickup at checkout. Track orders in real-time, manage delivery zones, and handle both self-delivery and third-party integrations.

Kitchen Order Display

Display incoming orders on kitchen screens in real-time. Organize by status, cooking time, and order type. Keep your kitchen running smoothly and efficiently.

Customer Loyalty Program

Build repeat business with points, rewards, and special offers. Encourage customers to order again with personalized promotions and birthday specials.
