Our Story
🍴

Rooted in Craft, Driven by Flavor

Ember & Oak started with a simple idea: what if every dish told the story of the land it came from? Chef Marcus Rivera and his team source every ingredient within 50 miles, working with local farmers and ranchers who share our obsession with quality.

Our open-flame kitchen isn't just for show — it's the heart of everything we do. From wood-fired steaks to ember-roasted vegetables, every plate carries the smoky, complex character that only real fire can deliver.
