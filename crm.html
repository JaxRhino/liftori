QUESTIONS, ANSWERED

When does my workspace go live?

Within 24 hours of checkout. Early access workspaces are activated by our team so every company starts with a clean, correctly configured setup for their trade.

Am I charged before my workspace is active?

Your subscription starts at checkout, but if we ever miss the 24-hour activation window, we'll credit your first month. During early access, activation is hands-on and fast.

What if my industry isn't listed?

Pick "Your Trade" and tell us what you do. The platform is built generically for service businesses — pipelines, jobs, crews, invoices — and we configure it to your workflow.

Is my data shared with other companies?

Never. Every company gets its own dedicated, isolated workspace and database. Your customers, pricing, and financials are yours alone.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Plans are month-to-month with no contracts. Cancel anytime from your workspace or by emailing support.

What's the difference between this and a custom build?

Liftori CRM is our ready-to-run platform for service businesses. If you need something fully custom — a different industry, a customer-facing app, a marketplace — check out Custom Builds.
