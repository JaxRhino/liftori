About & join

We're building the room we wish we'd had.

After Hours exists for one kind of person: the one with a day job and an idea, who's tired of doing it alone at midnight. If that's you, you're already one of us.

The story

It started with $61.

In 2021, Nora Bramwell opened a side shop while working full-time in logistics. First month: $61 in sales and a lot of doubt. What changed wasn't a course or a hack — it was a small group chat of other people building after work, trading honest advice and zero hype.

That group chat grew into a forum. The forum grew into After Hours. Today it's 14,200 members across nine hustle tracks, a shop and library built by the community, and a simple promise: real help from people one step ahead, never the get-rich-quick crowd.

Questions

The things people ask first.

Is it really free to join?
Yes. The Member tier is free forever — full access to every community track, the ability to post and ask questions, the Friday brief, and member pricing in the shop. Inner Circle ($19/mo) adds accountability pods, live clinics, and the full course library.
Do I need to already have a side hustle?
Not at all. A huge part of the community is people figuring out what to start. The "Pick your track" rooms and beginner courses are built exactly for the very first step.
What kinds of side hustles does this cover?
Nine tracks today: reselling and flipping, freelancing, print-on-demand, newsletters, digital products, vending, e-commerce, content and creator work, and local services. New tracks open as the community asks for them.
Is this another get-rich-quick thing?
No — and we're allergic to it. No income guarantees, no fake screenshots, no "passive income while you sleep." Members post real numbers, including the slow months. That honesty is the whole point.
What if I buy a product or course and it's not for me?
Every digital product, course, and book comes with a 14-day, no-questions refund. If it didn't help, you don't pay for it.
Can I cancel Inner Circle anytime?
Anytime, in one click. You keep your free Member account and any products or courses you bought outright.
